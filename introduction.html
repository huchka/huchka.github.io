Bio

Hello! My name is Khureltulga Dashdavaa. I am from Mongolia. I was born in June 9th 1987 in Khentii, Mongolia. I was always passionate about Technology and Science. Specially, I studied Physics, IT programming, and Maths in my childhood. So, I had been participated to national and international contests. I have received bronze medal prize in International Physics Olympiad.

I came to Japan to study my major as IT after my high school graduation in 2007. Since 2007, I am studying in Japan from bachelor to Ph.D. course. Currently, I am 2nd year of Ph.D. course. My major is IT, especially, my research is about High Performance Computing and Software Defined Networking.

Hobby
